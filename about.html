ABOUT US

Our History

Founded in 2010, Football Agency Sierra Leone has been at the forefront of developing and promoting Sierra Leonean football talent.

The agency started with a vision to create pathways for local players to reach international standards.

Over the years, it has successfully placed over 50 players in professional clubs across Africa, Europe, and Asia.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To become the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized for developing world-class Sierra Leonean football talent and creating sustainable career pathways for players.

Our Mission: To identify, nurture, and promote exceptional football talent from Sierra Leone through comprehensive training, professional development, and strategic international partnerships.
