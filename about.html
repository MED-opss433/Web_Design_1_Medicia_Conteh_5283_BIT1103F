OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2010, Football Agency Sierra Leone has been at the forefront of developing and promoting Sierra Leonean football talent. We started with a vision to create pathways for local players to reach international standards.

Over the years, we've successfully placed over 50 players in professional clubs across Africa, Europe, and Asia, becoming the most trusted football agency in Sierra Leone.

VISION AND MISSION

Our Vision

To become the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized for developing world-class Sierra Leonean football talent and creating sustainable career pathways for our players.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and promote exceptional football talent from Sierra Leone through comprehensive training, professional development, and strategic international partnerships.
